Sunwoo Kim is a Ph.D. student at Seoul National University(SNU) advised by Professor Jehee Lee. Also, he is co-advised by Professor Sehoon Ha since 2020 unofficially. He received a B.Sc from the Pohang University of Science and Technology. His main research interest lies on connecting computer animation to the robotics but not limited to the certain fields. He is craving for defining exciting research questions and solving it with various methods.
