Sunwoo Kim

1 June 2024 • 1 min read

Sunwoo Kim is a researcher in the Graphics AI Lab at NC Research. He received his master’s degree from Seoul National University (SNU) under the guidance of Professor Jehee Lee. He has also collaborated with Professor Sehoon Ha. He earned his B.Sc from the Pohang University of Science and Technology. His primary research interest is in bridging computer animation and robotics, though his work is not confined to these fields alone. He is passionate about identifying compelling research questions and addressing them using various methods.
