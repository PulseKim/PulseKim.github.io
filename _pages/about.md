Hi, I’m Sun Woo Kim, a PhD student in Computer Science at Simon Fraser University under the supervision of Professor [Xue Bin (Jason) Peng](https://xbpeng.github.io/). Before joining SFU, I fulfilled my military service as a Technical Research Personnel at the Graphics AI Lab at NCSOFT(later spun-offed to NC AI). I received my M.Sc. in computer science from Seoul National University advised by Professor [Jehee Lee](https://mrl.snu.ac.kr/~jehee/). I was fortunate to collaborate closely with [Sehoon Ha](https://faculty.cc.gatech.edu/~sha9/) during that time. I earned my B.S. degree in creative IT Engineering from POSTECH. My research lies at the intersection of robotics and computer animation.
